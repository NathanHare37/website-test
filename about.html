About Max:

Height: 6'0" (183cm)

Eye Color: Brown

Hair Color: Brown

Location: Sarasota, Florida
   As early as I can remember, voice acting has been a major influence on me. I was born in Sarasota Florida just outside of Orlando. My mom, being a huge fan, introduced me to the amazing world of Disney when I was young, and I quickly became a fan of animation. The Lion King in particular was a major influence on me, especially the character Timon. I would perform plays for my parents as the character Timon and mimic his voice and mannerisms. I never liked meeting Timon at Disney however, because I knew it wasn't really him. What stood out to me was that he didn't have his voice, and though I didn't fully realize it at the time, it stuck with me how a character's voice can be so essential to who they are.

   As I got older my love of voices and acting grew stronger. I started watching British comedians like Monty Python and had a pretty convincing British accent from a young age. I loved to make my family laugh with a Sean Connery or Arnold Schwarzenegger accent. I impressed my entire fourth grade class by doing accents and impressions for the class talent show. My parents saw how much I loved doing accents and decided to hire a dialect coach for me that I have worked with for years. In high-school I discovered Dungeons and Dragons and I suddenly found a new way to practice new voices. I instantly fell in love with creating characters with unique backstories and even more unique accents. To this day, every character I play in D&D has a unique voice to match their individual personalities.

   After high-school while trying to figure out what I wanted in life, I decided to make my passion into my career and become a voice actor. Since then I started taking private VO acting classes with Emmy award winner David Tom, an amazing acting coach. He has given me the tools I need to start my career, and with those tools I’ve been able to turn my dream into reality. This last year alone, I’m starring as the lead character in the highly anticipated new animated show called “Welcome to the Circus” which will premiere on YouTube in the near future. I specialize in character voices and regional dialects as well as audiobooks, commercials, trailers, internal corporate videos, and much much more. Check out my reels page for a sample of my work or contact me through any of the links on my contact page. Voice acting has always been my greatest passion and it would be my honor to use that passion to bring your characters to life.

About Max:

Height: 6'0" (171cm)

Eye Color: Brown

Hair Color: Brown

Location: Sarasota, Florida
   As long as I can remember I wanted to be a voice actor. One of my earliest memories is trying to memorize and copy all of Timon’s lines from The Lion King. My talent for my middle school talent show was accents and impressions. In highschool I got into D&D and loved giving my characters unique voices. As an adult I decided to try to make my passion into a career, but it’s still my greatest passion first and I’d love to use that passion to bring your character to life.

   I specialize in accents and character voices but I also do commercials, narration, audio books, internal corporate videos, pretty much anything you could ask for. Check out the reels tab to see some of what I can do.
